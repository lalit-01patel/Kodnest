THE RISE OF KODNEST

KodNest has emerged as one of India's leading ed-tech platforms focused on transforming aspiring students into industry-ready software professionals. Through structured training programs, real-world projects, and placement support, KodNest bridges the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

The platform emphasizes practical learning through intensive coding challenges, mock assessments, and robust project-based education tailored for fresh graduates.

INDUSTRY-ORIENTED LEARNING

KodNest collaborates with industry experts to design relevant learning paths. Students work on modern projects, participate in internal coding contests, and receive individual mentorship from experienced technical professionals.

This hands-on methodology ensures complete mastery over backend systems, frontend layout architectures, database administration, and continuous integration models.

OUR SPECIALIZED COURSES

We deliver elite training structures tailored for rapid career integration. Our primary specialized curriculums include:

  • Java Full Stack: Core Java, Advanced Java, Hibernate, Spring Boot, and Microservices architecture.
  • Web Development: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and modern frontend library structures like React.
  • Automation Testing: Software QA fundamentals, Selenium WebDriver, and automation framework setups.
